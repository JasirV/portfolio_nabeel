What is Digital Marketing?

Digital marketing encompasses all forms of internet-based marketing strategies. In today’s digital age, it is essential for businesses to leverage online platforms to connect with both existing and potential customers. Digital marketing includes various channels such as search engines, social media, email, and websites. When executed effectively, digital marketing can significantly enhance your brand’s visibility and drive growth. The internet is a powerful tool for reaching consumers, who spend considerable time online. By utilizing digital marketing strategies, businesses can engage with their target audience more efficiently and on a global scale
